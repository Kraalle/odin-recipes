Lasagna

Description

An absolutely delicious meal made from pasta, beef, and sauce. Just unbelievable.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook up the beef
  2. Place pasta and beef between each other in layers
  3. Pour on tomato sauce
  4. Add mozzerella cheese
  5. Place in oven at 400 degrees for 30 minutes
