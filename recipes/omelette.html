Omelette

Description

A wonderful breakfast dish. Incredibly quick to make.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Tomato
  • Bacon
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook up eggs in the pan
  2. Place tomato, bacon, and mozzerella cheese ontop of the eggs
  3. Pour on tomato sauce
  4. Cook for 10 minutes on medium heat
  5. Fold in half and serve
