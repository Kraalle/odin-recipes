Pizza

Description

A traditional italian dish. You cannot go wrong with pizza on a Friday night.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps

  1. Strech out the pizza dough until it becomes a circle
  2. Place the tomato sauce on top of the pizza dough
  3. Add your mozzerella & basil
  4. Cook for 10 minutes on at 400F in the oven
  5. Cut into even peices and serve
